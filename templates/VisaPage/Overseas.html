Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

A Work Visa is essential for legally working in a foreign country for a set duration. Typically, obtaining a Work Visa necessitates a job offer from an employer in the destination country. The employer sponsoring your Visa is also a crucial requirement. Additionally, passing a Labour Market Test is often a prerequisite before applying for a Work Visa. To successfully secure a Work Visa, meeting the specific eligibility criteria set by the respective country is mandatory.
